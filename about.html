About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineering undergraduate at the University of Pittsburgh's Swanson School of Engineering, where I'm also part of the Frederick Honors College. My passion lies in embedded systems, circuit design, and renewable energy technologies.

Throughout my academic journey, I've worked on projects ranging from microcontroller-based automation systems to power electronics simulations. I'm particularly interested in how electrical engineering can contribute to sustainable energy solutions, smart infrastructure, and accessibility.

When I'm not in the lab or studying, you can find me tinkering with Arduino projects, participating in engineering design competitions, or on the Ultimate Frisbee field.
